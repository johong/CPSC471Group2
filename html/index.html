  • — upload —

    Users are able to upload prerecorded audio files onto the database. The audio file will have the title, artist, length, genre, and track number information which will be displayed to the user. Users can also upload samples which are short clips of the audio file to give other users a preview of their song.

  • — create —

    Users will be able to create playlists by selecting songs. Playlists can be shared to the public or made private so that only the user who created it will be able to view and listen to it. All playlists contain a name, ID number, and the number of tracks.

  • — share —

    The online music database allows users to share their songs and playlists with other users. In order to do this, a user must upload a song or create a playlist and make it public. Once a song or a playlist is made public, other users can view or listen to it without further action from the uploader or creator.

  • — sort —

    By collecting data on artists, songs, albums, and recording studios, users will be able to sort through the music selection in the datababse. The user will then be able to organize their music database to their own liking.

  • — search —

    By collecting data about the songs, artists, albums, and recording studios in our database, users will be able to search through everything efficiently and easily.

  • — sample —

    Our database gives users the option of uploading a sample of an audio file to compliment the full audio. The sample will provide a short clip of the song to users. Users will be able to choose whether they want to upload a prerecorded sample of a song so not every song in our database will have a sample.
